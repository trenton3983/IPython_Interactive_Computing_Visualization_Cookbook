## Recipe 4

* [Stochastic differential equations](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stochastic_differential_equation).
* The [white noise](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/White_noise), derivative of the Brownian motion.
* The [Langevin equation](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Langevin_equation).

* [Itô calculus](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/It%C5%8D_calculus) generalizes differential calculus to stochastic processes.
* The [Euler-Maruyama method](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Euler%E2%80%93Maruyama_method) is a simple numerical method for simulating diffusion processes.
